Halo-Halo Dessert Recipe

This is a popular Filipino dessert especially during the summer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can jackfruit, drained and diced
  • 1 jar sweetened white kidney beans
  • 1 jar red (adzuki) beans, drained
  • shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup vanilla ice cream, or to taste

Steps:

  1. Place jackfruit in a bowl. Add white beans and red beans. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Place 2 tablespoons of the mixture in a tall glass.
  3. Fill glass 2/3-full of shaved ice.
  4. Pour evaporated milk over the shaved ice. Mix using a teaspoon.
  5. Top with 2 tablespoons vanilla ice cream.
  6. Repeat the steps above in three more glasses.

Cook's Notes:

You can make shaved ice using a blender, knife, or ice shaver. Adjust ice and water levels to get the desired consistency.
